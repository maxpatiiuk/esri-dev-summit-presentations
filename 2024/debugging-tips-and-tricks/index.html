ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript:
Tips and Tricks for Developing and Debugging Apps

Presenters: Max Patiiuk, Noah Sager

Slides & Notes: https://bit.ly/esri-2024-debugging-tips-and-tricks

(Max) Welcome everyone to the session on ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript!

My name is Max Patiiuk. I am a developer on the JavaScript team. I mostly work with Widgets and Web Components. And I am presenting with my colleague Noah Sager.

(Noah introduces himself)

(Max) Today we will provide you with tips and tricks for developing and debugging the apps you build.

This session contains important introductory material for those who are just getting started with developing mapping applications.

Throughout the presentation we will be linking to a number of external resources that provide supplemental information for those who wish to dive deeper. You can go over all of these resources by taking a look at our slides after the presentation. The link to our slides and resources is on the screen, and we will share it again at the end of the presentation.
