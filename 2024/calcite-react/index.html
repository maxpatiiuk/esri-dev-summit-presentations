Building Web Apps with Calcite Design System and React

Presenters: Ryan Libed, Max Patiiuk

Slides & Notes: https://bit.ly/esri-2024-calcite-react

(Ryan) Welcome everyone to the session about working with the Calcite Design System in React applications

My name is Ryan Libed. I am a developer on the ArcGIS online team. I am primarily focused on Instant Apps.

(Max) Hi! My name is Max Patiiuk. I am a developer on the JavaScript team. I mostly work with Widgets and Web Components.

(Ryan) Calcite Design System makes it easy for developers like you to build great looking web mapping applications with ease, while also providing good user experience and accessibility. Calcite empowers developers to focus on what is important - the content of your application, taking care of many design decisions for you.

That is why we are excited to see how you utilize Calcite in your applications. And to get you started, in this presentation we will show how to add Calcite Components to your React applications with just a few lines of code.
